Hi! I am Yafei Shi (施雅菲), a Master’s student in Technology Economics and Management at Central University of Finance and Economics (CUFE), advised by Prof. Shandong Mou.

My research focuses on healthcare operations management, vehicle routing and scheduling, and supply chain resilience. I develop optimization algorithms, including ALNS, Gurobi-based mathematical programming, and hybrid heuristics, for real-world logistics and emergency-response problems.

I am seeking PhD opportunities starting Fall 2027. I am especially interested in research groups working on operations management, healthcare logistics, supply chain resilience, and data-driven optimization.

Research Interests

  • Healthcare logistics optimization: mobile clinic cooperative routing for chronic disease screening in rural elderly populations, with demand decay and multi-vehicle synchronization.
  • Operations research and optimization: Adaptive Large Neighborhood Search (ALNS), mathematical programming, Gurobi, evolutionary game theory, and system dynamics.
  • Supply chain resilience: price prediction with Random Forest, LSTM, and graph neural networks; emergency response mechanism design.

Selected Projects

Designing Proactive Healthcare Networks with Mobile Clinics for Co-morbidities
